Cheers to 2026!

By KT | January 11, 2026

Hey everyone,
First things first, I want to apologize for the tardiness of this post! I know we're well into the swing of 2026. Life, as it often does, threw a few curveballs my way, and getting this written took a backseat for a little while. But better late than never, right? I've been so eager to share some reflections and hopes with you all, and I truly appreciate your patience.


Now that I’ve cleared the air (and hopefully, you’ve all forgiven my slight delay!), let's dive into what 2026 holds! I always find this time of year to be a great time to reflect on how much I’ve grown and how much I’ve learned over the past year. It’s a chance to look back at what worked, what didn’t, and how we can continue to grow.

I also use this time to set new goals for myself for the rest of the year. Because there is always something new to learn, I enjoy identifying areas where I can challenge myself to be a little better than I was the previous day.


For me, the focus this year is on embracing new skills. I've always been fascinated by CSS Frameworks and JavaScript Libraries; they make it much easier to solve common problems while saving a significant amount of time.

  • Tailwind CSS: I love how it provides low-level utility classes that act like building blocks (similar to LEGOs). For example, you can create smooth transitions and animations easily with classes like transition-transform and hover:scale-110.
  • React JS: This allows you to create a wide range of dynamic UIs, from single-page web apps to cross-platform mobile apps using React Native.

I'm committing to dedicating consistent time each week to dive into these tools. It's less about becoming an expert overnight and more about the joy of learning and expanding my horizons.


What about you? What are your hopes and dreams for 2026? Have you set any goals, or are you simply going to see where the year takes you? I'd love to hear your thoughts in the comments below. Whether you're planning grand adventures or small, daily improvements, every step forward is a victory.

Let's make 2026 a year of incredible progress, joy, and meaningful experiences.

Cheers to a fantastic year ahead!
